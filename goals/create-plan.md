The council applies the COM-B (Capability, Opportunity, Motivation–Behaviour) framework to structure the plan. Capability interventions address the knowledge and skills the customer needs to build. Opportunity interventions target the social and environmental conditions that can enable or constrain the desired behaviour. Motivation interventions strengthen the automatic and reflective drivers that sustain effort over time. The resulting 6-week plan organises these three levers into weekly milestones, each grounded in evidence retrieved from the corpus and calibrated to the specific profile gathered during intake.
